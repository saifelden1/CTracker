# CTracker Design System (Quick Reference)

## Color Palette

### Backgrounds
- `--background`: #1a1d24
- `--elevated`: #1f2229
- `--surface`: #252932
- `--surface-hover`: #2d323d
- `--sidebar`: #16181d

### Text
- `--foreground`: #e4e6eb
- `--muted`: #9ca3af
- `--subtle`: #6b7280

### Primary (Green)
- `--primary`: #10b981
- `--primary-hover`: #059669
- `--primary-muted`: #064e3b

### Borders
- `--border`: #2d323d
- `--border-strong`: #404854

### Status
- Success: #10b981 | Warning: #f59e0b | Error: #ef4444 | Info: #3b82f6

## Spacing & Sizing
- **Spacing**: xs=4px, sm=8px, md=16px, lg=24px, xl=32px, 2xl=48px
- **Radius**: sm=4px, md=6px, lg=8px, xl=12px
- **Typography**: 14px base, weights 400/500/600

## Component Patterns

### Buttons
- Primary: Green bg, white text
- Secondary: Gray bg
- Ghost: Transparent, hover only
- Destructive: Red

### Badges
- Small pills with 15% alpha bg + solid text color
- Variants: success/warning/error/info/default

### Cards
- Dark surface + subtle border + lg radius
- Hover: lighter bg + shadow

### Inputs
- Dark bg + border + green focus ring
